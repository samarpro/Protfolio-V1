About LFS

WHY CHOOSE US?

Established two decades ago, Little Flower School is a fast developing institution with an amazing growth statistics. It has changed the way knowledge is imparted by ever setting higher standards of quality education accompanied by intelligent innovations. Along with intellectual pursuit, we also inculcate high moral and ethical qualities in the students and thereby making them well disciplined and responsible persons in the family, society, and in the nation at large. The School aims at the integral development of every child to make him/her committed to the service of God and of Nepal, instilling in him/her a spirit of compassion, dedication to duty, love for fellowmen and a thirst for wisdom and excellence. Students are also taught to respect the cultural heritage of Nepal. The school will take every effort to form the students to be loyal and committed citizens of Nepal enabling them to render efficient and excellent service to the nation and fellowmen.
